Why is 7 the most common?

There are 6 ways to roll a 7 (1+6, 2+5, 3+4, 4+3, 5+2, 6+1), but only 1 way to roll a 2 (1+1) or 12 (6+6).

The closer a sum is to 7, the more combinations can make it. That's why the chart forms a pyramid shape!

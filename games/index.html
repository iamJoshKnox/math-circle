🎲 Math Circle Games

Fun math adventures for curious minds!

Week 1
🔍
Odd One Out
Spot the number that doesn't belong! Use your pattern-finding skills to find the oddball.
Week 2
🪞
Symmetry Mirror
Complete the pattern on the other side of the mirror! Place colored pegs to make it symmetric.
Week 3
🧐
Logic Deduction
Meet Gobr, Stoon, and friends! Use clues to figure out who is who. Think like a detective!
Week 4
🏗️
Tower of Hanoi
Move the stack of disks from one peg to another. Can you do it in the fewest moves?
Week 5
🎲
Dice Roller
Roll two dice and track the sums. Which number comes up most? Make a prediction and find out!
Week 6
✨
Magic Square Builder
Place numbers 1-9 so every row, column, and diagonal adds to 15. It's like a number puzzle!
Week 7
🌍
Euler Explorer
Count faces, vertices, and edges of 3D shapes and discover a magical formula!
Week 8
🔐
Code Breaker
Crack secret codes and decode hidden messages. Become a master codebreaker!
Week 9
🏠
Floor Plan Designer
Look at a 3D room and recreate it as a top-down floor plan. Think in new dimensions!

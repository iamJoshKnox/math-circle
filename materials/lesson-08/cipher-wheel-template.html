How to Assemble & Use Your Cipher Wheel

  1. Cut out the outer ring along the outer dashed circle line.
  2. Cut out the inner disk along its dashed circle line.
  3. Place the inner disk on top of the outer ring, lining up the center dots.
  4. Push a brass brad (paper fastener) through both center dots and flatten the prongs on the back.
  5. To encode: Choose a shift number (e.g., 3). Line up A on the inner disk with D on the outer ring. Now find each letter of your message on the outer ring, and write the matching inner ring letter as your secret code!
  6. To decode: Set the same shift, then find each coded letter on the inner ring and write the matching outer ring letter.
